How does it work?

ShoppingList is a web app that enables you and your friends living in the same household to keep track of your shopping needs. Simply add an article to the list and everybody will see it.

Benefits

  • Easily add articles to your shopping list
  • Swipe them off the list when bought
  • Get notified of other's actions
  • See what's happened in the past
  • View statistics of orders and purchases

Support

ShoppingList supports all devices that exist and have a browser.
